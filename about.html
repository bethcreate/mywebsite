BIOGRAPHY

I was born in December 1998 in a family of eleven siblings being the 10th child. I grew in a small town called Jambini, in Nyandarua county. I started schooling
at Munyaka primary school where I was able to achieve a good grade and continue
with my secondary school education. My High school life was fun and interesting because
of the friends I had and the effort I used to put in my academics which made me thrive in my performance. At the end of it, I graduated but I did not achieve whatever I intended to get
I really wanted to join a university and continue with my education but I
did not get that chance. As you all know our society value university more than anything else.

After high school my life was not easy for I had to look for away forward. I had to move from my parent's place to the city on my own
in order to look for greener pastures. Life got more complicated and I had no job nor support from my
Family. I could cry the whole day until I realized that was doing no good to me. I had to look for a solution
to whatever was happening around me.
I was lucky that I secured my first job which made me so happy for I could at least afford my basic needs. I did not know
that was very little money compared to the responsibilities that I had. I had to pay rent, buy food, renew my
closet and all that. At some point it got so hard that I could not even afford food, I could just drink some water
and hope for a better tomorrow .
